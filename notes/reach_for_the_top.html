Reach for the Top - Notes

📖 Summary – Part I (Santosh Yadav)

This part narrates the inspiring journey of Santosh Yadav, the first woman in the world to climb Mount Everest twice. Born in a conservative family in Haryana, she broke societal norms with her strong will and determination. She joined a mountaineering institute against her family’s wishes and later joined an Indo-Nepalese Everest expedition. She not only climbed Everest but also helped save fellow climbers, showing bravery and compassion.

📖 Summary – Part II (Maria Sharapova)

The second part tells the story of Maria Sharapova, a Russian tennis player who rose to the top of the tennis world. She left her home at a young age to train in the U.S., facing loneliness and hardship. Despite challenges, her determination and mental toughness led her to win Wimbledon at age 17. Maria’s journey reflects discipline, sacrifice, and the spirit to achieve greatness.

📑 Important Questions & Answers

Short Answer Questions (2 Marks)

A1: Santosh Yadav is the first woman to climb Mount Everest twice.

A2: She was born in a small village in Haryana.

Q3: What challenges did Santosh face in her early life?

A3: She had to fight against traditional norms and family opposition to pursue mountaineering.

Q4: What did Santosh do during her Everest expedition that showed her compassion?

A4: She saved the lives of fellow climbers by sharing oxygen and providing support.

Q5: How old was Maria Sharapova when she won Wimbledon?

A6: She left Russia to train in the U.S. at a young age to become a tennis champion.

A7: She faced loneliness, separation from family, and tough competition.

Q8: What qualities helped Maria reach the top?

A8: Her mental toughness, discipline, and hard work helped her succeed.

Q9: What does Maria say about her ambition?

A9: She says her ambition is to become the world’s number one tennis player and stay there.

Q10: How are Santosh and Maria similar?

A10: Both overcame hardships and showed determination to achieve success in their fields.

Long Answer Questions (5 Marks)

Q1: Describe Santosh Yadav’s achievements and personality.

A1: Santosh’s achievements include climbing Everest twice and saving lives during her expedition. She is courageous, determined, compassionate, and a role model for women worldwide.

Q2: Discuss the sacrifices Maria Sharapova made to achieve her dream.

A2: Maria sacrificed her childhood, left her family, and endured loneliness and tough training in a foreign land to achieve her goal. Her sacrifices paid off when she became a world champion at 17.

Q3: What similarities and differences do you find between Santosh Yadav and Maria Sharapova?

A3: Both faced struggles and societal challenges but differed in their fields—Santosh in mountaineering and Maria in tennis. Both are examples of resilience and dedication.

Q4: How does “Reach for the Top” inspire young readers?

A4: The chapter inspires readers to dream big, work hard, and break barriers. It shows that success requires sacrifice, determination, and courage.

Q5: Write a diary entry as Maria Sharapova after winning Wimbledon.

A5: *“Dear Diary, Today I fulfilled my dream. Winning Wimbledon at 17 proves all my sacrifices were worth it. I miss my family but feel proud of what I’ve achieved.”*
